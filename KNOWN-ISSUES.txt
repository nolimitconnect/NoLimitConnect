1.) On very old computers when playing video you hear sound but screen is just solid blue
Reason - Some video cards predate the shader programs and Graphics GPU support (Mostly Before 2005).
Mitigation - No plan to support these very old machines but you could add a modern video card.

2.) When debugging in Visual Studio the app may throw a exception when shutting down
Exception thrown at 0x00007FFA78EF9491 (amdxx64.dll) 
Reason - Qt does not release directx resources in the correct order or thread is not stopped in correct order.
Mitigation - This exception can be ignored
